**8. Which tool is better for competitor analysis?** 

Both tools excel in competitor analysis, but in different ways. **Semrush** is fantastic for in-depth keyword research and backlink analysis. **Similarweb** offers broader market intelligence, including traffic trends and audience interests, helping you understand your competitors‚Äô full digital strategy.
